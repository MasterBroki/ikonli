1. DevIcons

  • Font version: 1.8.0

  • http://vorillaz.github.io/devicons/

  • Cheat-Sheet

1.1. Installing

gradle
compile 'org.kordamp.ikonli:ikonli-devicons-pack:1.3.0'
maven
<dependency>
    <groupId>org.kordamp.ikonli</groupId>
    <artifactId>ikonli-devicons-pack</artifactId>
    <version>1.3.0</version>
</dependency>
